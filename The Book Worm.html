Welcome to the Bookworm! This website is designed to help you easily choose your next read. Everytime I read a book, I write a review right here on this site to help other readers spend their time reading meaningful books that they will enjoy.

First things first, you should probably know what genres of books I enjoy, which can be seen in the table below. The table provides the genre, a rating of the genre on a scale of 1 to 10, (in my opinion), my favorite book from that genre, and it's author.

I have been reading for as long as I can remember. Everytime that I finish a book, I place it in its rightful spot on my bookshelf, which is alphabetical order. Then, I fill out my reading journal to record my thoughts on the book before I forget. I normally read one or two books in a week, so needless to say, my bookshelf is overflowing. I like it this way however; I believe that it adds charm to my room and is interesting to look at. An empty bookshelf is like an empty box of goldfish: dusty and boring. So I have made a promise to myself to never stop reading, and I hope that one day I will have a library in my house that is filled from wall to wall with books.

If after you navigate my website, and give some of my suggestions a try, but do not agree with my opinions, try visiting www.bookcritics.org to see what they have to say.
